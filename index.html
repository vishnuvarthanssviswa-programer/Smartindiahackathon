About the Platform

Developed under the Smart India Hackathon (SIH25093) by the Government of Jammu & Kashmir to modernize student activity tracking in Higher Education Institutions (HEIs).

  • Centralized tracking of academic, co-curricular, and extracurricular achievements.
  • Secure validation and faculty feedback system.
  • Real-time analytics and reporting tools.
  • Data privacy and secure document handling.
  • Mobile responsive and multilingual support.
  • Future-ready with integration potential.
